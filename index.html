EDOBAN

Welcome to EDOBAN

Most people do know when their birthdays are, but some might not know what day of the week they were born.

Luckily EDOBAN is here. EDOBAN is a website application that calculates the exact day of the week you were born. To make it more interesting it generates your Akan name

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day-name' which corresponds to the day of the week they were born.


Here is a cheat sheet

The number tells you the number to the day you are born

Code

0: Sunday
1: Monday
2: Tuesday
3: Wednesday
4: Thursday
5: Friday
6: Saturday
